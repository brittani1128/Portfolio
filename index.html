A little bit about how I got into programming:

I attended the University of Texas (Hook em!) where I studied biology and then pursued a Masters degree in marine biology to later begin my career in environmental consulting. I’ve always been drawn to science and understanding how things work and fit together, from the brain and body to entire ecosystems. However, I still felt I hadn’t found the right career path for me, until I began programming.

I took the opportunity to learn coding while in a sales role with a tech company here in Austin. From there I knew I had to pursue a programming career and so I completed a fullstack developer program at UT. While I am new to the industry, I have been coding for over a year now and am looking for my first professional role.

I feel my strengths lie in the frontend where I have an eye for detail and design, and the freedom to be creative. Thus I enjoy working with frontend frameworks like React and learning the latest technologies to enhance user experience. However, I understand the backend and have experience building fullstack applications using Node, Express, and both MongoDB and MySQL. Ultimately, I'm hungry to learn and am therefore open to all opportunities in UX/UI, frontend, and backend.
